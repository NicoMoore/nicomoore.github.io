Hi, I'm Nico.

Welcome to my personal website. This is a place where I share what I'm working on, what I'm reading, and podcasts I'm creating.

I currently work at Epic Systems as a software implementer, leading the install of electronic health record software at hospitals and helping them transition off of their legacy systems. Outside of work, my main interests are economics and learning from history. I explore economics and learn from history primarily through books and through interviews I conduct for my podcast. One of my long-term goals is to become a venture capitalist. I love the idea of using capital to create a better future, as history has shown it to be one of the major drivers of positive societal advancement. I believe modernity has been propelled by capital coupled with the conviction that the future can be better than the present. It’s been said that the job of an investor is not necessarily to predict the future, but to see the present more clearly than others. I think history, and historical patterns, provide a lens to see the present as it actually is, instead of the most personally comfortable interpretation.
